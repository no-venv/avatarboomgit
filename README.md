**avatarBoom** is a plugin that allows you to easily create, and customize avatars through access to the catalog, and accessory modification tools

currently, avatarboom allows you to do the following:

<details>
<summary>› automatic rig detection & rig importer</summary>

**avatarBoom** utilizes `SelectionService` to automatically detect edible rigs. just select and get creating!
you can also import default rigs or other rigs from your friends!
> ⚠️ avatars from the toolbox is NOT supported, and probably will never be supported at all. most of the avatars/rigs in the toolbox has malformed `HumanoidDescription`, which may cause unexpected behavior. by default, avatarBoom will throw an error.
> 
> it's highly advised to use the built-in rig importer!!!
</details>

<details>
<summary>› built in catalog</summary>

 **avatarBoom** has a built in catalog, with a variety of filters to choose from. you are also to paste ids and equip them to your avatar!
